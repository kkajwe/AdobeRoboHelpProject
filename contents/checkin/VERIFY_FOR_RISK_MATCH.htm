Name = Name of Renter                    
================================================================
"VERIFY FOR RISK MATCH" means the customer may be on a list of 
 former renters with similar names to whom we will not rent.   
                                                               
- Check the information for each name on the list (bottom of the
  101 screen) to see if it matches your renter.  BE SURE TO PAGE
  FORWARD TO SEE ALL NAMES.                                    
                                                               
  If the name matches:  DO NOT RENT                            
                                                               
  If the name does NOT match:                                  
                                                               
  - Type 0 (zero) in the NAME field                            
  - Erase rest of name & press ENTER                             When a DO NOT RENT message is received, follow the instructions
 from Wizard and/or call your manager.                          
                                                                
  Examples:                                                     
                                                                
   DO NOT RENT - NO EXCEPTIONS - NOTIFY WHQ SECURITY            
   DO NOT RENT - OVERDUE PROBLEM - NOTIFY MANAGER
